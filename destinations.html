Popular Destinations

Explore India's most captivating travel destinations

Rajasthan - The Land of Kings

Experience royal grandeur in the desert state where majestic palaces, imposing forts, and vibrant culture create an unforgettable journey through India's regal history.

Rajasthan, India's largest state, is a treasure trove of architectural marvels, colorful traditions, and desert landscapes. From the pink-hued city of Jaipur to the golden fortress of Jaisalmer, every corner tells a story of valor, romance, and royal splendor.

Key Attractions

  • Jaipur: Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Amber Fort
  • Udaipur: Lake Palace, City of Lakes
  • Jaisalmer: Golden Fort, Desert Safari
  • Jodhpur: Mehrangarh Fort, Blue City

Goa - Tropical Paradise

Discover pristine beaches, Portuguese colonial architecture, and a laid-back atmosphere that makes Goa India's premier beach destination.

Goa, India's smallest state, offers a unique blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures. With its golden beaches, historic churches, spice plantations, and vibrant nightlife, Goa provides the perfect escape for both relaxation and adventure.

Must-Visit Places

  • North Goa: Baga Beach, Calangute, Fort Aguada
  • South Goa: Palolem Beach, Basilica of Bom Jesus
  • Old Goa: Se Cathedral, Church of St. Francis
  • Panaji: Latin Quarter, Mandovi River Cruise

Kerala - God's Own Country

Immerse yourself in the tranquil backwaters, lush hill stations, and rich cultural traditions of Kerala, where nature and spirituality converge.

Kerala, located on India's southwestern coast, is renowned for its network of backwaters, pristine beaches, and hill stations covered in tea and spice plantations. The state offers unique experiences from houseboat cruises to Ayurvedic treatments.

Top Experiences

  • Alleppey: Backwater cruises, Houseboat stays
  • Munnar: Tea plantations, Hill station retreat
  • Kochi: Chinese fishing nets, Historic Fort Kochi
  • Kumarakom: Bird sanctuary, Lake Vembanad
